Meat Recipes

Grilled Steak

Ingredients:
2 steaks (such as ribeye, sirloin, or New York strip), about 1 inch thick
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp paprika
Optional: Fresh herbs (rosemary or thyme) for garnish
Instructions:
1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
2. Mix olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika in a bowl to create a seasoning rub.
3. Pat steaks dry, then rub seasoning mixture evenly over each steak.
4. Grill steaks for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare doneness.
5. Check internal temperature with a meat thermometer; it should reach 130°F (54°C).
6. Remove steaks from grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
7. Optional: Garnish with fresh herbs before serving.
8. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!


Grilled Chicken Breast

Ingredients:
-4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
-2 tablespoons olive oil
-1 teaspoon salt
-1/2 teaspoon black pepper
-1 teaspoon garlic powder
-1 teaspoon paprika
-1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
-1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
Instructions:
1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
2. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, thyme, and oregano to make a marinade.
3. Rub the marinade over the chicken breasts, making sure to coat them evenly.
4. Place chicken breasts on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
5. Remove from grill and let rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!


Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon ginger, grated
2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
Cooked rice, for serving
Instructions:
1. In a bowl, marinate the sliced beef in soy sauce and sesame oil for 15-20 minutes.
2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
3. Add garlic and ginger to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
4. Add marinated beef to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes, until browned.
5. Add mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, until tender-crisp.
6. Serve beef stir-fry over cooked rice and enjoy!


Roast Chicken

Ingredients:

1 whole chicken (4-5 pounds)
2 tbsp olive oil
Seasoning blend: 2 tsp salt, 1 tsp each black pepper, garlic powder, paprika
1 lemon, halved
Optional: Fresh herbs
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Rinse, pat dry chicken.
3. Mix olive oil and seasoning blend.
4. Rub all over chicken.
5. Place lemon halves, herbs inside chicken.
6. Tie legs if desired, place in roasting pan.
7. Roast for 1 hour 15 minutes to 1 hour 30 minutes, until internal temp reaches 165°F (75°C).
8. Let rest 10 minutes, carve, serve.
