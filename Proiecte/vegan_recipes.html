Vegan Recipes

Banana Bread

Ingredients:

3 very ripe bananas, mashed
1/3 cup melted coconut oil
1/2 cup coconut sugar or brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups oat flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup chopped nuts or almonds (optional)
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease loaf pan.
Mix bananas, coconut oil, sugar, and vanilla.
Combine oat flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in another bowl.
Gradually add flour mixture to banana mixture; stir well.
Fold in nuts if using.
Pour batter into loaf pan; smooth top.
Bake 50-60 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool before slicing. Enjoy!


Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

1 cup quinoa, rinsed and drained
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can sweet corn, drained
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
1 small red onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
Juice of 2 limes
2 tablespoons olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let cool.
2. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, black beans, sweet corn, bell peppers, red onion, and cilantro.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Pour over salad and toss to combine.
4. Serve at room temperature or chilled. Optionally, garnish with sliced avocado.


Chickpea Curry

Ingredients:

2 cups cooked or canned chickpeas
1 large onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can coconut milk
2 tablespoons curry paste
1 teaspoon turmeric
1 tablespoon coconut or olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a large pot and cook onion and garlic until softened.
2. Add curry paste and turmeric, and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
3. Add diced tomatoes and coconut milk, stirring well.
4. Add chickpeas and simmer on low heat for about 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens and chickpeas are heated through.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve garnished with fresh cilantro. Serve with rice or naan bread.


Walnut Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

2 cups fresh basil leaves
1/2 cup walnuts
2 cloves garlic
1/4 cup nutritional yeast
1/4 cup olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
8 oz (225g) pasta of your choice
Instructions:
1. In a food processor, blend basil, walnuts, garlic, and nutritional yeast.
2. While blending, slowly add olive oil until smooth.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Cook pasta until al dente; reserve 1/2 cup cooking water.
5. Drain pasta and return to pot.
6. Toss pasta with walnut pesto, adding cooking water as needed.
7. Serve hot, garnished with walnuts and basil.
