Quick and Easy Recipes

Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

2 large tomatoes, sliced
1 ball fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
Fresh basil leaves
Balsamic glaze
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
1. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving platter, alternating them.
2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Serve immediately as a light and refreshing salad.


Stir-Fried Garlic Shrimp

Ingredients:

1 lb (450g) shrimp
4 cloves minced garlic
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
1 tbsp sesame oil
Salt, pepper to taste
Green onions, sesame seeds for garnish
Instructions:
1. Mix garlic, soy sauce, oyster sauce.
2. Sauté shrimp in sesame oil.
3. Add garlic mixture to skillet.
4. Toss shrimp in sauce.
5. Stir-fry until cooked.
6. Season, garnish.
7. Serve hot.


Spinach Lasagna

Ingredients:

2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas), chopped
1 tablespoon sesame oil
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
1 teaspoon minced garlic
Cooked rice or noodles for serving
Instructions:
1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add mixed vegetables and minced garlic, and stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
3. Stir in soy sauce and hoisin sauce, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
4. Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.


Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

2 slices whole grain bread, toasted
1 ripe avocado
Salt and pepper to taste
Optional toppings: sliced tomatoes, red pepper flakes, sesame seeds, or a drizzle of balsamic glaze
Instructions:
1. Halve and pit the avocado, then scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
2. Mash the avocado with a fork and season with salt and pepper.
3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto toasted bread slices.
4. Top with your favorite optional toppings, if desired, and serve immediately.
