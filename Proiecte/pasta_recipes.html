Pasta Recipes

Vegan Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients:
8 oz (225g) pasta of your choice
1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water for at least 4 hours or overnight
1 cup vegetable broth
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
2 tablespoons lemon juice
Salt and pepper to taste
Chopped fresh parsley for garnish
Instructions:
1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a blender, combine soaked cashews, vegetable broth, minced garlic, nutritional yeast, and lemon juice. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Transfer the cashew cream sauce to a saucepan and heat over medium heat until warmed through, stirring frequently.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Add cooked pasta to the saucepan and toss until well coated in the vegan Alfredo sauce.
6. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley.


Carbonara

Ingredients:
8 oz (225g) spaghetti
4 slices of bacon or pancetta, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 large eggs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
Salt and black pepper to taste
Chopped parsley for garnish
Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water according to package instructions until al dente. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
1. In a skillet, cook diced bacon or pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and a pinch of black pepper.
1. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the bacon and garlic. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour the egg mixture over the hot pasta, stirring constantly to coat the pasta evenly.
1. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of reserved pasta water to thin it out.
1. Season with salt and additional black pepper to taste.
1. Serve immediately with extra grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley for garnish.


Walnut Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:
2 cups fresh basil leaves
1/2 cup walnuts
2 cloves garlic
1/4 cup nutritional yeast
1/4 cup olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
8 oz (225g) pasta of your choice
Instructions:
1. In a food processor, combine basil leaves, walnuts, garlic, and nutritional yeast.
2. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
3. While the food processor is running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture forms a smooth paste.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta cooking water.
6. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
7. Add the walnut pesto to the pasta and toss until well coated, adding pasta cooking water as needed to reach desired consistency.
8. Serve hot, optionally garnished with additional chopped walnuts and fresh basil leaves.


Tomatoes and Basil Pasta

Ingredients:
8 oz (225g) pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
2 tablespoons olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
4-5 ripe tomatoes, diced
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves
Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)
Instructions:
1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
4. Add diced tomatoes to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes until they start to soften and release their juices.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine with the tomato mixture.
7. Stir in chopped fresh basil leaves and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until heated through.
8. Serve hot, optionally topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
