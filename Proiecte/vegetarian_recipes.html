Eggplant Parmesan

Ingredients:

2 medium eggplants, sliced into 1/4-inch rounds
Salt
2 cups marinara sauce
1 cup breadcrumbs
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
Fresh basil leaves for garnish
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Salt eggplant slices, let sit 30 minutes, then rinse and pat dry.
3. Prepare two bowls: one with beaten eggs, one with breadcrumbs and Parmesan.
4. Dip eggplant slices in eggs, then coat with breadcrumb mixture.
5. Arrange on baking sheet and bake 15-20 minutes until golden.
6. Spread marinara sauce in baking dish; layer half of eggplant slices.
7. Top with more sauce and shredded mozzarella. Repeat layering.
8. Bake 20-25 minutes until cheese is melted.
9. Garnish with fresh basil before serving. Enjoy!


Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

4 large bell peppers, halved and seeds removed
1 cup cooked quinoa
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup corn kernels
1 cup diced tomatoes
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon cumin
Salt and pepper to taste
1 cup shredded cheese (optional)
Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
3. Stuff each bell pepper half with the quinoa mixture and place in a baking dish.
4. If using cheese, sprinkle it over the stuffed peppers.
5. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes until peppers are tender.
6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.


Spinach Lasagna

Ingredients:

9 lasagna noodles
2 cups marinara sauce
2 cups chopped spinach
1 cup ricotta cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried basil
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
1. Cook lasagna noodles per package instructions; drain.
2. Sauté spinach, garlic until tender; season with salt, pepper, dried basil.
3. Mix ricotta with salt, pepper in a bowl.
4. Spread thin layer of marinara in baking dish; place 3 noodles on top.
5. Layer half of spinach, half of ricotta over noodles.
6. Repeat layers with marinara, noodles, spinach, ricotta.
7. Top with marinara, sprinkle mozzarella, Parmesan.
8. Cover with foil; bake 30 mins. Remove foil; bake 10 mins more until cheese melts.


Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1/4 cup vegan mayonnaise
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped red onion
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
Salt and pepper to taste
8 slices whole grain bread
Lettuce leaves and tomato slices for serving
Instructions:
In a large bowl, mash chickpeas with a fork or potato masher.
Add vegan mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, celery, red onion, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
Toast whole grain bread slices if desired.
Spread chickpea salad onto bread slices and top with lettuce leaves and tomato slices.
Serve sandwiches immediately.
