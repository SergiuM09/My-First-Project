Pizza Recipes

Pizza Pepperoni

Ingredients:

1 pre-made pizza dough
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
20-30 slices pepperoni
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Red pepper flakes (optional)
Fresh basil leaves (optional)
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Roll out pizza dough and place on baking sheet.
3. Spread sauce over dough, top with mozzarella, then pepperoni.
4. Sprinkle Parmesan and optional red pepper flakes.
5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
6. Garnish with basil if desired.
7. Slice and serve hot.


Pizza Margherita

Ingredients:

1 pre-made pizza dough
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
Fresh basil leaves
Salt and pepper to taste
Olive oil for drizzling
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Roll out dough and place on baking sheet.
3. Spread sauce over dough, then sprinkle with cheese.
4. Top with fresh basil leaves.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Drizzle with olive oil.
7. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
8. Slice and serve hot.


Hawaii Pizza

Ingredients:

Ingredients:
1 pre-made pizza dough
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup diced pineapple
1/2 cup diced ham or Canadian bacon
Optional: diced red onion, sliced black olives
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Roll out dough and place on baking sheet.
3. Spread sauce over dough, then top with cheese.
4. Add pineapple and ham (and optional toppings).
5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
6. Slice and serve hot.


Veggie Supreme Pizza

Ingredients:

1 pre-made pizza dough
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
Assorted veggies (bell peppers, mushrooms, red onions, black olives, cherry tomatoes, artichoke hearts, spinach)
Optional: crushed red pepper flakes, dried oregano, fresh basil leaves
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Roll out dough and place on baking sheet.
3. Spread sauce over dough, then sprinkle with cheese.
4. Top with assorted veggies.
5. Optionally, sprinkle with red pepper flakes and oregano.
6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
7. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
8. Slice and serve hot.
